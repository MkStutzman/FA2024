Horses are majestic and intelligent animals known for their strong bond with humans. Domesticated for thousands of years, they have played vital roles in agriculture, transportation, and sport. With around 400 distinct breeds, horses vary in size, color, and temperament, making them suitable for diverse activities, from racing and dressage to trail riding and working on farms. Their social nature means they thrive in herds, exhibiting complex behaviors and communication skills. Beyond their utility, horses also serve as companions, offering therapeutic benefits to many individuals. Their grace and beauty continue to captivate people worldwide.
